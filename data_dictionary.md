# Mutual Fund Data Dictionary

## amfi_code

Type: TEXT
Business Definition: Unique identifier assigned by AMFI to each mutual fund scheme.
Source: 01_fund_master.csv

## scheme_name

Type: TEXT
Business Definition: Name of the mutual fund scheme.
Source: 01_fund_master.csv

## fund_house

Type: TEXT
Business Definition: Asset Management Company (AMC) managing the mutual fund scheme.
Source: 01_fund_master.csv

## category

Type: TEXT
Business Definition: Broad classification of mutual fund schemes such as Equity, Debt, Hybrid, etc.
Source: 01_fund_master.csv

## sub_category

Type: TEXT
Business Definition: Detailed classification within a fund category.
Source: 01_fund_master.csv

## nav

Type: REAL
Business Definition: Net Asset Value per unit of the mutual fund scheme.
Source: 02_nav_history.csv

## date

Type: DATE
Business Definition: Date associated with NAV values or investor transactions.
Source: 02_nav_history.csv, 08_investor_transactions.csv

## transaction_type

Type: TEXT
Business Definition: Type of mutual fund transaction such as SIP, Lumpsum, or Redemption.
Source: 08_investor_transactions.csv

## amount_inr

Type: REAL
Business Definition: Transaction amount in Indian Rupees.
Source: 08_investor_transactions.csv

## kyc_status

Type: TEXT
Business Definition: KYC verification status of the investor.
Source: 08_investor_transactions.csv

## expense_ratio_pct

Type: REAL
Business Definition: Annual expense ratio charged by the mutual fund scheme.
Source: 07_scheme_performance.csv

## return_1yr_pct

Type: REAL
Business Definition: One-year return percentage generated by the mutual fund.
Source: 07_scheme_performance.csv

## return_3yr_pct

Type: REAL
Business Definition: Three-year return percentage generated by the mutual fund.
Source: 07_scheme_performance.csv

## return_5yr_pct

Type: REAL
Business Definition: Five-year return percentage generated by the mutual fund.
Source: 07_scheme_performance.csv

## alpha

Type: REAL
Business Definition: Measure of excess return generated by the fund relative to its benchmark.
Source: 07_scheme_performance.csv

## beta

Type: REAL
Business Definition: Measure of volatility of the fund relative to the market.
Source: 07_scheme_performance.csv

## sharpe_ratio

Type: REAL
Business Definition: Risk-adjusted performance metric of the mutual fund.
Source: 07_scheme_performance.csv

## aum_crore

Type: REAL
Business Definition: Assets Under Management measured in crore rupees.
Source: 03_aum_by_fund_house.csv
